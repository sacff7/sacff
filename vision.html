Vision

To have a God’s family of believers experiencing a life-changing, personal, and intimate relationship with Jesus through exaltation, edification and evangelism.

Exaltation:

Glorify God, magnify His name, worship Him in truth and spirit, and to express our love to God through a caring and functioning fellowship that nurtures loving relationships with God as well as fellow brothers and sisters.

Edification:

Grow in Christ together with fellow Christians reviving our hearts and minds with renewed spirit, experience a life-changing, personal, and intimate relationship with Jesus, while edifying one another for the glory of God and the good of His people.

Evangelism:

Serving Him with our heart and soul, encouraging fellow brothers and sisters to exhibit Christ-like behavior, contributing to spread the gospel to the lost, equipping his people for works of service, and building the body of Christ.

Mission

Our SACFF’s mission is to continue to build a Bible-based and Christ-centered fellowship, growing in Christ, nurturing loving relationships, exhibiting Christ-like behavior, and contributing to spread the gospel. Specifically, our activities are to

  • ➤   Build a family of believers who will love Him and honor Him
  • ➤  Draw closer to Jesus by developing a loving relationship with Him
  • ➤  Commit ourselves to Christ in truth and spirit and allow Him to fulfil His purpose in our lives
  • ➤  Share Christ’s love and passion with other Christians
  • ➤  Emphasize personal relationship with God
  • ➤  Encouraging commitment and care to each other within the fellowship
  • ➤  Strive to increase faith and spread the Word for His glory
  • ➤  Create a desire for worship and prayer in our families
  • ➤  Promote friendly and spiritual associations with brothers and sisters in Christ

Psalm 1 (KJV)

Blessed is the man that walketh not in the counsel of the ungodly, nor standeth in the way of sinners, nor sitteth in the seat of the scornful. But his delight is in the law of the LORD; and in his law doth he meditate day and night. And he shall be like a tree planted by the rivers of water, that bringeth forth his fruit in his season; his leaf also shall not wither; and whatsoever he doeth shall prosper. The ungodly are not so: but are like the chaff which the wind driveth away. Therefore the ungodly shall not stand in the judgment, nor sinners in the congregation of the righteous. For the LORD knoweth the way of the righteous: but the way of the ungodly shall perish.
