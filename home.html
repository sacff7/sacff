SACFF Monthly Service Schedule

SACFF meets three times a week. Sometimes more than three times if there are any special occations or festivals or celebrations.

  • Intercessory Prayers — First Saturday 7pm - 9pm

    We meet every first Saturday evening at 7pm for intercessory prayers. Please join us to pray for each other and to pray for our nation, world, salvation of the unsaved, etc.,


    "Again I say unto you, That if two of you shall agree on earth as touching any thing that they shall ask, it shall be done for them of my Father which is in heaven." - Matthew 18:19-20

  • Bible Study — Second Saturday Evening 7pm - 9pm

    Every second Saturday evening 7pm we meet to study God's word. One of SACFF members lead the bible study.

    “How sweet are thy words unto my taste! yea, sweeter than honey to my mouth!” — Psalms 119:103

  • Praise And Worship Service — Fourth Saturday 7pm-9pm

    Every forth Saturday we meet to praise and worship our Almighty God, Lord God Jesus Christ.

    “Let every thing that hath breath praise the LORD. Praise ye the LORD.” — Psalms 150:6

Psalm 1 (KJV)

Blessed is the man that walketh not in the counsel of the ungodly, nor standeth in the way of sinners, nor sitteth in the seat of the scornful. But his delight is in the law of the LORD; and in his law doth he meditate day and night. And he shall be like a tree planted by the rivers of water, that bringeth forth his fruit in his season; his leaf also shall not wither; and whatsoever he doeth shall prosper. The ungodly are not so: but are like the chaff which the wind driveth away. Therefore the ungodly shall not stand in the judgment, nor sinners in the congregation of the righteous. For the LORD knoweth the way of the righteous: but the way of the ungodly shall perish.
